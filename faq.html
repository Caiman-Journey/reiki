Caiman, also written as cayman, is a type of crocodile from Central and South America. Like all other members of the order Crocodilia, Caimans are amphibious carnivores that live along the edges of rivers and other bodies of water.

Alligators and Crocodiles are ancient animals that have a symbolism of teaching us how to tune into your innate skills and abilities. They have a close affinity to the Earth Mother in that this spirit also protects those under its guidance. Crocodile is not easily fooled and brings a lesson of patience, particularly during new beginnings.

As a spirit animal, the Caiman brings ancient knowledge and power to grow and transform. It has an intense symbolism for those who seek to dive deep into self discovery.

So the Caiman Journey is the journey of each one of us who are focused on fearlessly diving deep into self knowledge, growth and ascension, stepping into its innate power fiercely.
