Reiki Healing and Treatments for Everyone

Reiki is a Japanese therapy that promotes healing by bringing in life force energy to stimulate the body's innate capability of self regulating and healing. It improves sleep and relaxes the body, affecting positively all levels: physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual and it can be done in humans, pets and plants, in person or at a distance.

Who I am

Hi I am Carol, a brazilian mother of 2 beautiful girls living in Montreal. For as long as I can remember my biggest wish was always to help others. I had this growing intuition telling me always to seek a path where I could be of help. I have studied Anatomy in University to understand better our body, but something was missing. I knew there was more than the eyes can see, I could feel it. So in 2016 I decided to take my first Reiki course. It changed my life so much that I wanted to share this gift with others. And so I kept with my studies, diving deeper into my spiritual guidance to lead to the best way to help others. And now, after my latest Reiki course, I got attuned in the beautiful Holy Fire® Reiki energy, and I am super excited to share it with everyone. My goal is to be a channel of this beautiful energy to allow you to find your Soul path, balance your life and unlock your full potential! Let’s raise the World’s energy, by raising our own, one person at a time!
